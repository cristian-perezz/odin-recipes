Ingredients

  • 1 lb. Pizza Dough
  • 1 cup Tomato/Pizza Sauce
  • 12 oz. Mozzarella Cheese
  • Toppings of Choice.

Instructions

  1. Preheat Oven: Set oven to 450-500 degrees Fahrenheit with baking sheet inside.
  2. Mix Dough:Flour your surface, and being rolling/stretching the dough to your desired size and girth for the pizza.
  3. Transfer Dough:Transfer dough to parchement paper, then moving over to hot pan.
  4. Add Toppings:Begin spreading sauce, then sprinkle on the Cheese and add your toppings of choice.
  5. Bake:Almost Done! From here you may place the pizza in the oven and allow it to bake for 10-15 minutes until crust becomes golden and cheese appears melted.
  6. Enjoy:Now you may slice the pizza and serve as desired.
